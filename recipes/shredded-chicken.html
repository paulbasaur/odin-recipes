Shredded Chicken

Description

Perfectly shredded chicken.

Ingredients

  • 2 pounds boneless skinless chicken thighs
  • 1 cup chick broth
  • salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Combine all ingredients in instant pot.
  2. Lock lid and seal vent.
  3. Cook chicken on high pressure for 10 minutes.
  4. Let instant pot naturally depressurize.
  5. Shred chicken with forks.
